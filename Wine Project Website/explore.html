A Quantitative Comparison Analysis of Spanish Wine Scores vs Weather Data.

Is there a correlation between temperature, precipitation and wine scores?

After perusing through many datasets on various websites, our group decided that we would first attempt to find a dataset in 'JSON' format since we found that there were fewer complete datasets in 'JSON' than '.csv'. The next difficulty was trying to find two datasets that would have a similar data field that we could merge our two datasets on. We decided that we could use wine reviews with weather data, and then join the two dataset on year.

First our group pulled our wine data in JSON format from Kaggle's wine review dataset. Second our group pulled the weather data in .csv format from Data World"s Spanish weather dataset.
